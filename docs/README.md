## What is ANVIL?

ANVIL trains AI-displaced workers in 7 days to become AI-augmented specialists in three "boring niches":
1. **Benefits navigation** — Finding unclaimed benefits, denied coverage, overlooked tax credits
2. **Medical billing advocacy** — Auditing medical bills, fighting overcharges, negotiating with providers
3. **Small business compliance** — Navigating permits, licenses, regulations, payroll taxes

**The transformation:**
- **Before:** Unemployed, scared, competing with AI for shrinking jobs
- **After:** Earning $400-600/month (part-time) helping neighbors with AI-powered services
- **Timeline:** 7 days training → 30-90 days to first income

### Three Niches Explained

**1. Benefits Navigation**
- **What:** Finding unclaimed benefits, denied coverage, tax credits, government programs
- **Who needs it:** Low-income families, seniors, veterans, disabled individuals, anyone overwhelmed by bureaucracy
- **Value:** $8,400 average found per client
- **Earning:** $40-75/hour

**2. Medical Billing Advocacy**
- **What:** Auditing medical bills for errors, overcharges, upcoding; negotiating with providers and insurance
- **Who needs it:** Anyone with a medical bill over $500 (80% have errors)
- **Value:** $3,200 average savings per client
- **Earning:** $50-100/hour

**3. Small Business Compliance**
- **What:** Navigating permits, licenses, regulations, payroll taxes, safety requirements
- **Who needs it:** Small businesses (1-50 employees), solo entrepreneurs, new business owners
- **Value:** $5,800 average penalties avoided per client
- **Earning:** $60-100/hour

---

### The ANVIL Formula

**Problem:** AI eliminating jobs faster than traditional retraining can respond

**Solution:** 7-day training to become AI-augmented specialist

**How:** AI does 80% of analysis, human does 20% (verify + communicate + execute)

**Outcome:** 30-90 days to first income, $400-600/month part-time, $2K-3.5K full-time

**Impact:** Workers go from displaced to essential; communities get $8K+ value per client
