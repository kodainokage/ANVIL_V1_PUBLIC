## Template Usage Notes

**For Workforce Boards:**
- Emphasize: Completion rate, employment rate, cost per participant, WIOA alignment
- Include: Demographics, credential attainment, wage gains
- Downplay: Community impact numbers (they care more about participant outcomes)

**For Community Partners (Libraries, Nonprofits):**
- Emphasize: Community impact ($X found for clients), participant success stories
- Include: Geographic reach, clients served in their area
- Downplay: Cost metrics (they're not paying)

**For Funders/Investors:**
- Emphasize: ROI (community and participant), scalability, efficiency metrics
- Include: Cost per outcome, payback period, growth trajectory
- Downplay: Individual stories (they want numbers more than narratives)

**For Internal/Public Reports:**
- Balance: Mix of numbers and stories
- Emphasize: Total community impact, transformation stories, what's next
- Include: Challenges (transparency builds trust)
