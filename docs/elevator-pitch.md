They go from unemployed to earning $400-600 a month helping their neighbors find unclaimed money or fight overcharged bills. Last quarter, our people found $1.2 million for clients."

## 60-Second Pitch (Workforce Development Board)

**For government meetings, funding requests, partnership proposals**

> "ANVIL is a 7-day training program that turns dislocated workers into AI-augmented specialists in three boring but essential niches: benefits navigation, medical billing advocacy, and small business compliance.
>
> We're WIOA-eligible. Cost is $2,400 per participant — that covers everything: curriculum, AI tools, certification, job placement support.
>
> Our outcomes last quarter: 347 participants, 68% completion rate, 61% employed or self-employed within 90 days, median earnings $520 per month. That's part-time. Many go full-time within 6 months.
>
> The credential is ANVIL certification, recognized by 12 workforce boards across Texas, California, and Florida. But the real proof is their portfolio — the clients they serve, the money they find, the testimonials they earn.
>
> We can take cohorts of 25-30 per sprint. We run sprints every other week. First sprint can start within 14 days of approval.
>
> What questions can I answer?"

**Metrics they care about:**
- Completion rate (68% vs 40% typical for 6-month programs)
- Employment rate (61% vs 50% national average)
- Cost per participant ($2,400 vs $8K-15K bootcamps)
- Time to employment (90 days vs 6-12 months)
- Credential attainment (100% of completers get certified)

**Objections you'll hear:**

**"7 days seems too fast."**
→ "We're not teaching them to be the expert. We're teaching them to use expert-level AI tools. The AI does 80% of the analysis. They learn to verify, communicate, and execute."

**"How do we track self-employment outcomes?"**
→ "We have them report monthly for 6 months with payment platform screenshots. 90% comply. We only count verified earnings."

**"What if they don't finish?"**
→ "68% finish. The 32% who don't usually have childcare emergencies or internet access issues. We offer makeup sessions. Even with that, our completion rate beats most programs."

## The Formula

Every pitch, regardless of length, follows this structure:

1. **PROBLEM** — People are losing jobs to AI (emotional hook)
2. **SOLUTION** — 7-day training, AI-augmented specialist (concrete action)
3. **PROOF** — Real numbers (completion rates, earnings, impact)
4. **ASK** — Clear next step (call, workshop, enrollment)

**What to NEVER say:**
- "Empower" (meaningless)
- "Leverage" (corporate slop)
- "Synergy" (nobody cares)
- "Innovative" (prove it, don't claim it)
- "Transform lives" (show it with numbers)

**What to ALWAYS include:**
- Specific numbers (7 days, $520/month, 61% employment)
- Human impact (clients served, money found)
- Clear next step (call, link, enrollment)
- Why it's different (AI-augmented, not competing with AI)

## Objection Handling Cheat Sheet

**"7 days seems too short."**
→ "We're teaching them to use AI tools, not to be the expert themselves. It's like learning to drive a car, not build one."

**"How much do people really earn?"**
→ "Median is $520/month part-time within 90 days. Range is $200-1,200 depending on hours worked. Many go full-time within 6 months at $2K-3.5K/month."

**"What if nobody hires them?"**
→ "They don't need to be hired. They're self-employed service providers. They get clients through Google Business, word of mouth, community referrals."

**"Is there really demand for this?"**
→ "Americans leave $140 billion in unclaimed benefits on the table every year. Medical billing errors affect 80% of hospital bills. Small businesses pay $12 billion in avoidable compliance penalties. The demand is enormous."

**"What if AI replaces them too?"**
→ "AI is the tool they USE, not the competitor. As AI gets better, they get more powerful. They're the human interface for AI-powered services."

**"Why haven't I heard of this before?"**
→ "Because it's new. Benefits navigation as a profession has existed for years, but training programs have been expensive and slow. We made it fast and free by using AI to do the heavy lifting."
