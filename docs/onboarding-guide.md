# ANVIL Onboarding Guide

How to get started with ANVIL, step by step, for each user type.

---

## For Individuals (Self-Enrollment)

You want to learn a new skill and start earning. Here's your path.

### Step 1: Take the Career Match Quiz (3 Minutes)

**Go to:** anvil-training.org/quiz

**What happens:**
- 8 questions about your situation, skills, and goals
- No right or wrong answers — just helping us match you to the right niche
- Takes 3 minutes

**What you'll get:**
- Your top 2 niche matches (benefits navigation, medical billing advocacy, or small business compliance)
- Explanation of why each niche fits you
- Real examples of what you'd do
- Earning potential for each niche

---

### Step 2: Review Your Match and Choose Your Niche (5 Minutes)

**Read the results carefully.**

Each niche match shows:
- What the work involves (day-to-day tasks)
- Who your typical clients would be
- Real case examples
- Earning range ($40-100/hour depending on niche and experience)
- Why we matched you to this niche based on your quiz answers

**Pick the niche that sounds most interesting to you.**

You're not locked in — you can train in a second niche later. But start with one.

---

### Step 3: Lock in Your Spot (2 Minutes)

**Click: "Start Your 7-Day Sprint in [Niche]"**

**Fill out the form:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Current situation (just lost job / unemployed 1-3 months / unemployed 3+ months / employed but need extra income)
- How you heard about ANVIL

**Submit.**

No credit card required. No payment info. Just your contact details.

---

### Step 4: Check Your Email (Within 5 Minutes)

**Subject: "[Your Name], you're in. Your 7-day sprint starts [Date]."**

**The email contains:**
- Confirmation of your spot
- Start date (next Monday, or 2 weeks from now if this week's cohort is full)
- Link to join the private Signal group (your cohort + alumni)
- Link to the orientation video (8 minutes, watch before Day 1)
- Sprint Prep Guide (PDF with what to expect each day)

**Action items:**
- Move this email to your Primary inbox (check spam)
- Join the Signal group (you'll get support from instructors + other students)
- Watch the orientation video
- Mark Day 1 on your calendar

---

### Step 5: Prep for Day 1 (30 Minutes, Optional but Recommended)

**Watch the orientation video (8 minutes):**
- Overview of the 7-day sprint
- What you'll learn each day
- How to succeed (show up, do the assignments, ask questions)
- Meet your instructors

**Read the Sprint Prep Guide (10 pages):**
- Day-by-day breakdown
- Assignment descriptions
- Tools you'll use (all web-based, no downloads needed)
- FAQ

**Introduce yourself in the Signal group:**
- Post: "Hi, I'm [Name], joining from [City]. Lost my job as [previous role], excited to learn [niche]. Looking forward to Day 1!"
- You'll get welcomes from instructors and other students
- This is your community for the next 7 days (and beyond)

---

### Step 6: Complete the 7-Day Sprint

**See the full day-by-day breakdown in the Product Walkthrough.**

**High level:**
- **Day 1-4:** Learn the niche, the AI tools, the workflows, the communication skills
- **Day 5:** Set up your professional presence (Google Business, Calendly, payment)
- **Day 6:** Get your first FREE clients (for portfolio building)
- **Day 7:** Take the certification exam

**Each day:**
- 60-90 minutes of live instruction (optional, recorded for async viewing)
- 60-90 minutes of assignments (hands-on practice)
- Support available via Signal group (response time: 2-4 hours)

**Total time commitment:** 2-3 hours per day for 7 days = 14-21 hours total

---

### Step 7: Pass the Certification Exam (Day 7)

**Format:**
- 10 practical scenario questions
- Multiple choice + short answer
- Open book, open AI tool (you'll use the tools in real work, so you can use them on the exam)
- 90-minute time limit
- Need 70%+ to pass

**Sample question:**
> Client is a single parent, works part-time ($28K/year), has one child in daycare ($600/month). What's the first benefit category you'd investigate and why?

**Results:**
- Emailed within 2 hours
- Pass = digital certificate + ANVIL Alumni Network access
- Fail = retake once for free, or request 1-on-1 coaching session

**Pass rate:** 82%

---

### Step 8: Get Your First 3 FREE Clients (Week 2)

**Why free?**
- You need portfolio pieces (testimonials, case studies)
- You need practice with real humans (not just practice scenarios)
- You need proof to show future paying clients

**Where to find them:**
- Post in your neighborhood Facebook group: "Just completed training as a [niche] specialist. Offering 3 free assessments this week to build my portfolio. Who wants in?"
- Post on Nextdoor
- Ask friends/family if they know anyone who needs help
- Check with your church, library, community center

**What to say:**
> "I just completed certification as a [benefits navigator / medical billing advocate / compliance specialist]. I'm offering 3 free assessments this week to build my portfolio and get testimonials. Interested?"

**Typical response:** 5-10 inquiries. Pick 3, schedule them via Calendly.

**After each session:**
- Deliver your findings (written report + 20-minute explanation call)
- Ask for a testimonial: "If you found this helpful, would you mind writing a short review for my Google Business Profile?"
- Post the testimonial (with permission)

---

### Step 9: Get Your First PAID Client (Week 3-4)

**Where to find them:**
- Word of mouth from your free clients ("My neighbor helped me find $800 in benefits — here's her number")
- Google Business Profile (people searching "benefits navigator near me")
- Nextdoor (offer paid services, mention you're certified)
- Local Facebook groups (post your services, link to testimonials)
- Library/community center bulletin boards (flyer with your Calendly link)

**What to charge:**
- Start at $40-60/hour (or $50 flat fee for a basic assessment)
- After 5 paid clients, raise to $60-75/hour
- After 10 paid clients, raise to $75-100/hour

**First paid client checklist:**
- [ ] They book via Calendly
- [ ] You send them intake form (template provided by ANVIL)
- [ ] They fill it out before the session
- [ ] You run their info through AI tools
- [ ] You meet via Zoom (or phone, or in-person if local)
- [ ] You deliver findings + written report
- [ ] They pay via Stripe/PayPal/Venmo
- [ ] You ask for testimonial

**You just earned your first dollar as an ANVIL specialist.**

---

### Step 10: Build Your Practice (Month 2-3)

**Month 2 goals:**
- 8-12 paid clients
- 6+ testimonials on Google Business Profile
- Refine your workflow (you'll get faster)
- Raise your rates after 5 clients

**Month 3 goals:**
- 12-15 paid clients
- Start getting referrals (past clients send you new clients)
- Consider affiliate partnerships (refer tax pros, attorneys, financial advisors for a fee)
- Decide: Is this a side income or could it be full-time?

**Support during this phase:**
- ANVIL Alumni Network (Signal group stays active)
- Monthly office hours with instructors
- Access to updated AI tools and templates
- Quarterly refresher trainings

---

### Step 11: Decide Your Path (Month 4-6)

**Option A: Keep it part-time**
- 10-15 clients/month
- $800-1,200/month income
- 15-20 hours/month time investment
- Stable side income while you job search OR while you work another job

**Option B: Go full-time**
- 40-60 clients/month
- $2,500-4,000/month income
- 30-40 hours/week time investment
- Build a real business (LLC, business bank account, hire an assistant)

**Option C: Specialize further**
- Focus on ONE subcategory (e.g., veteran benefits only, or medical billing for cancer patients only)
- Charge premium rates ($100-150/hour)
- 20-30 clients/month
- Become the known expert in your niche

**Option D: Train in a second niche**
- Add medical billing advocacy if you started in benefits navigation
- Offer bundled services
- Increase your addressable market

**ANVIL supports all four paths.** There's no "right" answer. Do what fits your life.

## For Workforce Development Boards (WIOA Partnership)

You want to add ANVIL to your ETPL (Eligible Training Provider List) and start referring participants.

### Step 1: Initial Contact (15-Minute Call)

**Email us:** partnerships@anvil-training.org
**Or call:** (512) 555-0199

**In the email, include:**
- Your name and title
- Your county/region
- Number of dislocated workers you serve per quarter
- Your ETPL approval process
- Preferred call times (we'll send a Calendly link)

**On the call, we'll cover:**
- ANVIL program overview (7 days, 3 niches, outcomes data)
- Cost: $2,400 per participant (covers everything)
- Your current WIOA priorities and whether ANVIL fits
- Timeline for ETPL approval
- Pilot cohort options (start small before full commitment)

**Outcome:** You'll know if ANVIL is a fit. We'll know if we can meet your requirements.

---

### Step 2: Review Program Materials (1 Hour)

**We'll send you:**
- Full curriculum (day-by-day breakdown)
- Outcomes data (completion rates, employment rates, wage gains)
- Cost breakdown ($2,400 covers what)
- ETPL application template (pre-filled for your state)
- Sample participant testimonials
- Q1 2026 impact report

**Review these with your team.**

**Common questions at this stage:**

**Q: How do you track self-employment outcomes?**
A: Monthly earnings reports + payment platform screenshots for 6 months. 90% of participants comply. We only report verified earnings.

**Q: What if participants don't have reliable internet?**
A: We partner with libraries and community centers to provide in-person access. Or we refer them to your in-person programs.

**Q: Can you handle 50+ referrals per quarter?**
A: Yes. We run cohorts of 25-30 every 2 weeks. Excess participants roll to the next cohort (max 2-week wait).

**Q: What's your refund policy if someone drops out?**
A: If they drop out in the first 2 days, full refund. After Day 2, no refund (but they can rejoin a future cohort at no cost).

---

### Step 3: Submit ETPL Application (We Help)

**We provide:**
- Pre-filled application template for your state
- Required documentation (curriculum, instructor credentials, outcomes data)
- Liability insurance certificates
- WIOA compliance attestations

**You do:**
- Review the application
- Submit to your state ETPL system
- Follow up with your state workforce agency if they have questions

**Timeline:**
- ETPL approval typically takes 30-60 days
- We can run a pilot cohort while you wait for approval (self-funded participants or your discretionary funds)

---

### Step 4: Promote to Your Participants

**Once approved, we'll provide:**
- Flyer template (print or digital)
- Social media posts (for your Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- Email template (send to your participant list)
- Script for case managers (how to explain ANVIL to participants)

**Your case managers should say:**
> "We have a new program that trains you in 7 days to become a benefits navigator [or medical billing advocate or compliance specialist]. It's free, it's fast, and 61% of graduates are earning within 90 days. Interested?"

**Referral process:**
- Case manager refers participant to ANVIL
- We send participant the intake form
- Participant confirms eligibility (WIOA or other funding)
- Participant enrolls in next available cohort (max 2-week wait)

---

### Step 5: Track Outcomes (We Report Quarterly)

**What we track:**
- Enrollment (how many referred, how many started)
- Completion (how many finished all 7 days)
- Credential attainment (how many passed certification exam)
- Employment (how many employed/self-employed at 30, 60, 90 days)
- Earnings (median monthly income at 30, 60, 90 days)
- Client impact (total $ found/saved by your participants for their clients)

**How we report:**
- Quarterly report (Excel + PDF summary)
- Participant-level data (anonymized or with consent)
- Success stories (2-3 testimonials per quarter)

**You use this for:**
- WIOA annual reports
- Board presentations
- Grant applications
- Showing your community you're delivering results

---

### Step 6: Scale or Adjust

**After 1-2 quarters, you'll know:**
- Is ANVIL delivering the outcomes we promised? (Completion rate, employment rate, wage gains)
- Are participants satisfied? (Testimonials, complaints, feedback)
- Is it cost-effective? ($2,400 per participant vs outcomes achieved)

**Options:**
- **Scale up:** Refer more participants, add ANVIL to your "recommended programs" list
- **Adjust:** Change which participants you refer (e.g., focus on long-term unemployed, or those with transportation barriers)
- **Pause:** If outcomes aren't meeting your standards, pause referrals and we'll investigate

**Our commitment:** If we're not delivering the outcomes we promised, we'll refund your last quarter of enrollments and figure out what went wrong.

## Common Questions Across All User Types

### "Is ANVIL really free?"

**For individuals:** Yes, if you're WIOA-eligible (lost your job, or low-income). Your local workforce board pays the $2,400. If you're not WIOA-eligible, we run free intro cohorts periodically (funded by grants or employer partnerships). Sign up for the waitlist.

**For workforce boards:** You pay $2,400 per participant. That's the full cost. No hidden fees.

**For employers:** You pay $2,400 per employee (volume discounts available).

---

### "What if I don't finish the 7 days?"

You can retake the sprint one time at no cost. If you drop out again, you're not eligible for future cohorts (we need to prioritize people who will complete).

---

### "What if I pass the exam but don't get clients?"

We provide 90 days of job placement support:
- Help finding your first 3 free clients
- Feedback on your marketing (Google Business Profile, Nextdoor posts, flyers)
- Monthly check-ins
- Access to ANVIL Alumni Network (peer support)

If after 90 days you still haven't gotten a single client, we'll do a 1-on-1 session to diagnose what's wrong (usually it's marketing, not skills).

---

### "Can I train in more than one niche?"

Yes. Complete one niche first, get some clients, THEN come back for a second niche. We offer alumni discounts (50% off for second niche).

---

### "What if I don't have a computer?"

You need internet access and a device (computer, tablet, or even a smartphone for some parts). If you don't have one:
- Check if your local library has laptops you can borrow
- Ask your workforce development case manager about device loan programs
- Check with ANVIL — we have a limited number of loaner Chromebooks for participants who need them

---

### "Is this a job or self-employment?"

Both are possible.

**Self-employment (most common):** You find your own clients, set your own hours, keep 100% of what you earn.

**Employment:** Some nonprofits, tax firms, law firms hire ANVIL-certified specialists as employees. We'll introduce you to employers in our network who are hiring.

---

### "How long does it take to start earning?"

**Typical timeline:**
- Day 7: Certification
- Week 2: First 3 free clients (for portfolio)
- Week 3-4: First paid client
- Month 2: 8-12 paid clients, $500+ earned
- Month 3: 12-15 paid clients, $700+ earned

---

### "What if I live in a rural area?"

The training is fully online, so location doesn't matter. For getting clients:
- Rural areas often have HIGHER demand (less competition, more underserved populations)
- You can serve clients via phone or Zoom (doesn't have to be in-person)
- Partner with local libraries, churches, community centers to reach clients

---

### "Do I need a business license or LLC?"

Not initially. You can operate as a sole proprietor (just using your own name). After you're earning consistently ($1,000+/month), consider:
- Business license (required in some cities/states)
- LLC (protects personal assets)
- Business bank account (separates personal and business finances)

ANVIL provides a guide to these steps in your Month 3 materials.
