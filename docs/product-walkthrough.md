### The Call: Validation

**James's questions:**

**Q: "How do you ensure quality with a 7-day program? Most training is 6+ months."**

A: "We're not teaching them to BE the expert. We're teaching them to USE expert-level AI tools. The AI does 80% of the analysis. They learn to verify, communicate, and execute. It's like learning to drive a car, not build one."

**Q: "What's the credential? Is it recognized?"**

A: "ANVIL certification. It's currently recognized by 12 workforce boards across 3 states. We're working on national accreditation. But honestly, the real credential is their portfolio — the testimonials, the Google reviews, the clients served. That's what gets them hired or self-employed."

**Q: "How do you track self-employment outcomes? That's always messy for us."**

A: "We have them report monthly for 6 months. We verify via payment platform data (Stripe, PayPal). If they report $500/month earnings, we ask for a screenshot of their dashboard. 90% provide it. For the 10% who don't, we don't count them as employed."

**Q: "What's your completion rate and why?"**

A: "68%. That's high for us because it's only 7 days — hard to drop out. The 32% who don't finish usually have one of three issues: childcare emergency, no internet access, or they realized it's not for them after Day 2. We offer makeup sessions for the first two groups."

**Q: "What does $2,400 per participant cover?"**

A: "Everything. Curriculum, AI tool access for 90 days, 1-on-1 support, alumni network, certification exam, job placement support. No hidden fees."

**Q: "How many can you take from my county per quarter?"**

A: "We can handle cohorts of 25-30. If you send more, we add a second cohort. We run sprints every other week."

**James's decision:** "Send me the ETPL paperwork. I'll put you in front of the board next month."
