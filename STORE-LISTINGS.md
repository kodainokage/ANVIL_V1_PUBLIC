### Full Description

AI is replacing millions of jobs. You probably already know that. What you might not know is that there are boring, essential jobs where AI does the heavy lifting but a human is still required -- and almost nobody is filling them.

ANVIL teaches you three of those jobs in 7 days:

BENEFITS NAVIGATION
Help people find money they're owed. SSDI, SNAP, Medicaid, tax credits -- Americans leave $140 billion in unclaimed benefits on the table every year. AI does the research. You verify it, explain it, and file the paperwork. Average found per client: $8,400.

MEDICAL BILLING ADVOCACY
80% of hospital bills have errors. AI scans the bill, flags the overcharges, and drafts the dispute letter. You make the call, negotiate the reduction. Average savings per client: $3,200.

SMALL BUSINESS COMPLIANCE
Small businesses pay $12 billion a year in avoidable fines because nobody told them about a permit or a deadline. AI tracks the regulations. You file the forms and keep them legal.

HOW IT WORKS
- Take a 3-minute career quiz to find your best niche
- Complete the 7-day training sprint (online, self-paced, with live support)
- Get your first free clients to build your portfolio
- Start charging. Most graduates earn $400-600/month part-time within 90 days.

The training is free. We're funded by workforce development boards and employer partnerships. Your success is our business model -- if you don't land work, we don't get paid.

WHO THIS IS FOR
- Anyone who lost a job to AI or automation
- Anyone who needs income now, not in 6 months
- Anyone who wants to help their community while getting paid
- Career changers who want flexible, remote-friendly work

WHAT YOU GET
- 8-question career match quiz
- Full 7-day curriculum with read-aloud support
- Practitioner playbooks for each niche
- Client deliverable templates (ready to use day 1)
- Completion certificate
- Referral program to earn while you learn
- Works offline (PWA -- install on your home screen)

No subscriptions. No upsells. No bait-and-switch. Take the quiz, do the work, get paid.
