# ANVIL -- One-Page Executive Summary for Workforce Development Boards

## AI Navigation & Vocational Integration Layer

---

## The Problem

Artificial intelligence is displacing workers faster than the workforce development system can retrain them. Administrative assistants, data entry clerks, customer service representatives, and other mid-skill workers are losing positions to automation, and the existing pipeline of 6-month certificate programs and multi-year degree pathways cannot respond at the pace displacement is occurring.

Meanwhile, millions of Americans need professional help navigating government benefits, disputing medical bills, and keeping small businesses compliant -- services that are underserved, high-demand, and resistant to full automation because they require human judgment, empathy, and verification that AI cannot provide.

These two problems are the same problem. The workers being displaced can fill the service gap -- if they are trained to use AI as a tool rather than compete with it.

---

## The Solution

ANVIL is a **7-day, 19-hour intensive training program** that transforms displaced workers into AI-augmented specialists in three career pathways:

1. **Government Benefits Navigation** -- Helping individuals identify and apply for programs they qualify for (SNAP, Medicaid, LIHEAP, WIOA, SSI/SSDI, and 15+ additional programs)
2. **Medical Bill Auditing** -- Finding errors on medical bills (present in approximately 80% of hospital bills), negotiating reductions, and connecting patients to financial assistance
3. **Small Business Compliance** -- Identifying every permit, license, and registration a small business needs at the federal, state, county, and city level

Participants learn to use freely available AI tools to handle 70--90% of the research and analysis, then add the human skills AI cannot replicate: client intake, output verification, ethical judgment, and plain-language communication.

---

## Why It Works

- **AI handles the research.** Participants do not need to memorize regulations, billing codes, or program eligibility rules. AI retrieves and organizes that information. Participants verify it and deliver it to clients.
- **7 days is enough.** The program is not teaching participants to be attorneys, doctors, or accountants. It teaches them to be specialists at the intersection of AI capability and human necessity -- a narrower, faster skill to acquire.
- **The services are needed now.** Every community has residents who miss benefits they qualify for, patients drowning in medical debt, and small business owners confused by compliance. These are not speculative markets.
- **Graduates earn income quickly.** The program includes client acquisition training. Graduates are expected to serve their first client within 14 days of completion.

## Cost to the Board

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Requested WIOA reimbursement per participant | $2,000--3,000 |
| Actual program cost per participant | Approximately $600 |
| Cost to participant | $0 |
| Duration | 7 days (19 hours of instruction) |

**For comparison:** The average WIOA Individual Training Account is $3,000--5,000 for programs lasting 3--12 months. ANVIL delivers measurable outcomes at a fraction of the cost and time.

---

## What the Board Gets

- **Trained, certified specialists** serving constituents in the board's service area
- **Benefits navigation services** that help residents access programs they are already entitled to, increasing the community's utilization of existing resources
- **Medical billing advocacy** that reduces financial burden on residents and reduces uncompensated care costs in the healthcare system
- **Small business support** that helps new businesses launch compliantly and existing businesses avoid violations
- **Outcome data** reported quarterly in the format required by the state, including employment rates, earnings, and credential attainment
- **A cost-effective use of WIOA funds** with per-participant costs 50--85% below comparable programs
